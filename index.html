About Me

I’m a self-taught Software Engineer with 1 year of hands-on experience in web development, primarily focused on building scalable and efficient backend systems. My core expertise lies in Laravel, PHP, JavaScript, and Ajax, with a strong command over building and integrating RESTful APIs. I have developed full-fledged systems like real estate platforms, learning management systems, and intelligent recognition apps — all tailored to solve real-world problems. While backend development is my forte, I’m deeply passionate about emerging technologies like AI, Machine Learning, and building SaaS-based solutions that serve practical business needs. I thrive on clean architecture, data handling, and turning business logic into working code.

What I Offer

I build full-featured web apps using Laravel, PHP, and MySQL, following clean architecture and modern coding standards. From authentication to dynamic interfaces, I manage both backend and frontend using Blade, JavaScript, and AJAX for seamless performance.

Backend Engineering

I focus on clean logic, performance tuning, and database optimization using Laravel, PHP, and MySQL. My approach ensures stability, scalability, and flexibility for complex systems like SaaS platforms and custom business tools.
